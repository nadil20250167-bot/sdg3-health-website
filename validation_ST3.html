Reflection on the Validation Process

Running my pages through the W3C validator early caught two common issues before they piled up: an unclosed <div> in the feedback form and a missing alt attribute on one gallery-style image. Fixing these early made later edits much faster, since I wasn't debugging structural errors and styling issues at the same time. The main lesson was to validate after each new section rather than waiting until a page felt "finished."
