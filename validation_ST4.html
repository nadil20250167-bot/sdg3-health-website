Reflection on the Validation Process

Running my pages through the W3C validator early on helped me catch a few minor issues before they piled up. I mostly had to fix a missing alt attribute on an image and an unclosed tag that I completely missed while writing the SVG. Fixing them was quick since the validator pointed directly to the exact line. The main takeaway for me was that just eyeballing my own code isn't enough, as the validator catches small details that are easy to skim past. Once those quick fixes were made, all three pages came back completely clean, aside from a few minor warnings.
